About Swami vivekanand public school

Established in the fond memory of Vijay Bhurthala the father of the Founder and Director of Swami vivekanand public school , it offers coaching for entrance examination of various competitive examinations like NDA, Navy, Airforce, Army, SSC, HSSC, DSSSB, KVS, All TET, Police, NVS, Bank Exam. This campus is a venture by her to cater to the demands and expectations of the students aspiring for competitive exams and focuses to be the best coaching centre.

Swami vivekanand public school also provides the unique facility of Test Series to its students so that they can hone their skills anytime they want.

They are the only coaching centre to have its own publication house, i.e. Swami vivekanand public school Publication, the most trusted name among students.
